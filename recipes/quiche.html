Easy Quiche Recipe

Description

Try this recipe for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. Loaded with broccoli, ham, parmesan cheese!

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups Milk
  • 4 Large Eggs
  • 1 Cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 3/4 Cup Biscuit Baking Mix
  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 10 Ounce Package Chopped Broccoli
  • 8 Ounces Cheddar Cheese
  • 1 Cup Cubed Cooked Ham

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Beat milk, eggs, parmesan cheese, baking mix, and butter in a large bowl
  3. Stir in broccoli, cheddar cheese, and ham
  4. Pour into quiche dish
  5. Bake in oven until eggs are set, about 50 minutes
