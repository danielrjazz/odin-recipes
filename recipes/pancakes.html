Easy Pancakes Recipe

Description

Nice easy pancake recipe that doesn't require much early-morning thought.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Milk and Oil
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Combine dry ingredients
  2. Mix in wet ingredients
  3. Pour batter into oiled pan
  4. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook until done
