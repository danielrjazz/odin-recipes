Easy Guacamole Recipe

Description

A nice easy guacamole recipe that is quick and simple to make. Great with chips or tortillas

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 ripe tomato
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1 lime

Steps

  1. Mash avocado
  2. Stir in onion, tomato, and garlic
  3. Season with lime juice, salt, and pepper
  4. Cover and chill for 30 minutes
